1. Purpose of the Service

IN ID Verify is a service that allows users to verify a digital identity remotely through:

  • Scanning of ICAO-compliant identity documents (e.g., passports and id-cards)
  • Performing a liveness detection check

2. Eligibility

You may use this service only if you:

  • Are at least 18 years of age
  • Possess a valid ICAO-compliant document
  • Have access to a smartphone with camera and biometric capabilities
  • Consent to the processing of personal data as described below

3. Identity Verification Process

The verification process includes:

  • Capturing images of your identity document (e.g., passport or national ID)
  • Running an NFC chip read (if supported)
  • Taking a selfie and performing a liveness check
  • Matching your face against the document photo using facial recognition

The verification may be automated and/or reviewed by a human operator.

4. Authentication and Usage

Once your identity is verified, a mobile credential is issued and stored securely on your device. You may use this credential to:

  • Authenticate to reach online demo services
  • Prove specific identity attributes (e.g., age, name) during login

5. Data Collection and Privacy

We collect and process the following personal data:

  • Biographic data (name, DOB, document number)
  • Facial images and video (for liveness detection)
  • Device metadata

All data is processed under the principles of data minimization, purpose limitation, and security, in compliance with applicable data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR).

Data is:

  • Used solely for identity verification and authentication
  • Stored only temporarily in this demo version
  • Not shared with third parties, except for necessary service providers

You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your identity and data, including:

  • Encrypted communications
  • Secure biometric processing
  • Local credential storage with device-level protection (e.g., Secure Enclave, Keystore)
